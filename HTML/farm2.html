Farming

  • Aggregation
    • The foundation will work with different groups on the aggregation of their agricultural products related to them. The goal is to accumulate the products so that the prices favor small scale producers

  • Methods
    • The foundation will work with different groups, teaching them modern methods for increasing their products.
  • Storage
    • The foundation will work with different groups on the storage requirements for their products to reduce losses that occur post production.
  • Disease
    1. Plants

      2. The foundation will educate various groups on various plant diseases and how to mitigate losses arising from the diseases.

    2. Animals
      3. The foundation will educate various groups on various diseases that affect animals and how to reduce losses arising from diseases
